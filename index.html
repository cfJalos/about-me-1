A Guessing Game About Me


My name is Christopher Quiocho. I am originally from Honolulu, HI and I currently reside in Seattle, WA. I moved to Washington for a great job opportunity at the time and also because I absolutely love to go camping during the summer and skiing in the winter. The Pacific Northwest is great for that!

I was a Regional Talent and Business Development Manager for a well known hairdressing company for 9 years. I had the opportunity and pleasure of traveling and facilitating continuing education classes and on-going training throughout the United States, Canada and Latin America.

As far as education goes, I hold a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Political Science and a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Dance Theatre. I am currently attending Code Fellows to pursue a Certificate in Software Development.
